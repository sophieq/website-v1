About Me

I'm a second year Computer Science student at the University of Waterloo with a passion for programming, design and empowering women. All these activities gave me the opportunity to hone my creativity and use it to build something that was my own. I realized that when I grew up, I wanted to use my creativity to build something that would truely make an impact on people's lives. Now I know that programming was the perfect choice...simple and elegent yet powerful enough to change people's lives.
